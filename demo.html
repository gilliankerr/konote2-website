Try It

See how participants register for programs through KoNote2's self-service intake form.

How Self-Service Registration Works

Staff create a registration link, share it with participants, and review submissions — all without paper forms or manual data entry.

Staff Create a Link

Choose which program, which fields to collect, and set an optional deadline or capacity limit.

Participants Fill Out the Form

Participants visit the link (or it's embedded on your website) and submit their information securely.

Staff Review and Approve

Submissions appear in the dashboard. Staff can approve, waitlist, or merge with existing records.

About this demo

This form is running on a live KoNote2 demo instance. Any information you enter is for demonstration purposes only. The form fields, layout, and branding are all customisable by each agency.

What Staff See

When a participant submits the form, staff see the submission in their dashboard and can take action.

Duplicate Detection

KoNote2 automatically checks for matching names and email addresses, so staff can merge with an existing record instead of creating a duplicate.

Customisable Fields

Each registration link can include different field groups — contact info, demographics, emergency contacts, consent forms, and more.

Capacity and Deadlines

Set a maximum number of registrations or a closing date. The form automatically closes when limits are reached.

Embeddable

Embed the form on your agency's website with a simple iframe code, or share the direct link by email or social media.
